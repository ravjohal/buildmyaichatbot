## What You Can Do

With BuildMyChatbot.Ai, you can:

- **Create AI Chatbots** - Build intelligent chatbots that understand and answer customer questions
- **Train with Your Content** - Import knowledge from your website, documents, and PDFs
- **Customize Everything** - Match your brand with custom colors, logos, and personality
- **Capture Leads** - Collect visitor contact information automatically
- **Live Agent Handoff** - Take over conversations when human support is needed
- **Team Collaboration** - Invite team members to handle live chats
- **Track Performance** - See analytics on conversations, satisfaction, and more
- **Integrate with CRM** - Automatically send leads to Salesforce, HubSpot, or any CRM

## Quick Overview

Building your first chatbot takes just a few minutes:

1. **Name Your Chatbot** - Give it a name that reflects your business
2. **Add Knowledge** - Import content from your website or upload documents
3. **Customize** - Set colors, add your logo, and define the personality
4. **Configure Support** - Set up live agent handoff and lead capture
5. **Embed** - Add a simple script to your website
6. **Launch** - Your chatbot is live and ready to help customers!
